Our Services

Digital Land Services

Streamlined services available for citizens without visiting government offices.

Mutation Certificate

Transfer land ownership legally and update digital records instantly after registration.

Encumbrance (EC)

Verify the property history and ensure it is free from legal dues or mortgages.

Pattadar Passbook

Apply for new e-Passbooks, duplicate passbooks, or request data corrections.

Survey Services

Request official boundary verification, subdivisions, and physical land inspections.

Platform Features

Advanced Platform Features

Built with modern technology to ensure transparency, security, and speed.

Automated Fraud Detection

The system automatically cross-references Aadhaar, ownership data, and OCR-extracted document details to prevent identity and land theft.

GIS Boundary Mapping

Integration with spatial data allows officials to detect land encroachment and overlapping boundaries visually and programmatically.

Verifiable Certificates

Every issued document contains a cryptographic QR code, allowing third-parties (banks, buyers) to instantly verify authenticity online.

Automated Workflows

Applications are intelligently routed to the least-burdened officer in the correct jurisdiction, drastically reducing processing times.
